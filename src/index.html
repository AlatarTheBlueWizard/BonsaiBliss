Purpose/Theme

Zen is a Japanese word and it emphasizes meditation, the joy of life and living with simplicity. Zen Garden site takes us to an Asian environment that lead us to read the text, meditate the words and process its teachings in our minds. Bonsai trees help us see the longevity of life with its beauty and strength, while the rocks remind us to be strong and steadfast in achieving our goals. The site is designed to target people who wants to learn with open mind, in a peaceful environment. This is Zen Garden.
